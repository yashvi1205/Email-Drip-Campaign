> A 3-email referral outreach sequence for when a mutual connection
> has explicitly offered an introduction or suggested you reach out.

## Email 1: The Referral Drop (Day 1)

**Goal:** Lead with the referral name to earn immediate trust and establish credibility.

**Subject Line Options:**
- `[REFERRER_NAME] suggested I reach out`
- `Introduction from [REFERRER_NAME]`
- `[REFERRER_NAME] thought we should connect`

**Body:**

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

[REFERRER_NAME] suggested I get in touch with you. [REFERRAL_CONTEXT —
explain the connection naturally. Examples:
- "We were discussing [TOPIC] and they mentioned that [COMPANY_NAME]
   is working on [SPECIFIC_INITIATIVE]."
- "They spoke highly of the work you're doing at [COMPANY_NAME]
   and thought there might be a good fit."
- "After seeing what we helped [REFERRER_COMPANY/PROJECT] with,
   they thought you'd find this relevant."
1-2 sentences.]

A bit of context on why they made the connection: at [YOUR_COMPANY],
we help [TARGET_PERSONA] [ACHIEVE_OUTCOME]. [REFERRER_NAME] thought
this could be relevant because [SPECIFIC_REASON — tie to the prospect's
known situation].

[ONE_PROOF_POINT — brief credibility builder, e.g., "We've helped
companies like [EXAMPLE_1] and [EXAMPLE_2] achieve [RESULT]."]

Would you be open to a brief call this week to see if there's a fit?
Happy to work around your schedule — here are a few options:
- [OPTION_1]
- [OPTION_2]

Or grab any time here: [CALENDAR_LINK]

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]

P.S. Thanks again to [REFERRER_NAME] for connecting us!
```

**CTA:** Direct meeting request — referrals justify a faster ask.
**Timing:** Send within 24 hours of receiving the referral (freshness matters).

## Email 2: The Follow-Up (Day 4)

**Goal:** Add context and value without being pushy — respect the referral relationship.

**Subject Line Options:**
- `Re: [PREVIOUS_SUBJECT]` (keep thread)
- `Following up — [REFERRER_NAME] introduction`

**Body:**

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I wanted to follow up on my note from earlier this week — I know
inboxes get busy.

To add a bit more context on why [REFERRER_NAME] thought we should
connect:

[ADDITIONAL_VALUE — expand on the value proposition with something
specific and relevant. Options:
- Share a brief case study or result from working with a similar company
- Mention a specific insight about their industry or company
- Reference a resource that addresses their likely challenge
2-3 sentences.]

I'd genuinely love to hear about [COMPANY_NAME]'s approach to
[RELEVANT_TOPIC] — even if it turns out we're not a fit, I think
the conversation would be valuable.

Would 15 minutes work sometime next week?

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
```

**CTA:** Lower the commitment — 15 minutes, even if not a fit.
**Timing:** Morning, same timezone.

---

## Email 3: The Reconnect (Day 10)

**Goal:** Gentle final touch that preserves the relationship and leaves the door open.

**Subject Line Options:**
- `Re: [PREVIOUS_SUBJECT]` (keep thread)
- `Checking in, [FIRST_NAME]`

**Body:**

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I completely understand if now isn't the right time — I don't want
to overstay my welcome in your inbox.

If [RELEVANT_CHALLENGE/INITIATIVE] becomes a priority down the road,
I'd be glad to pick up the conversation. You can always reach me
at [YOUR_EMAIL] or book time here: [CALENDAR_LINK].

In the meantime, I'll let [REFERRER_NAME] know we connected (or tried to!).
Wishing you and the [COMPANY_NAME] team a great [QUARTER/SEASON].

Cheers,
[YOUR_NAME]
```

**CTA:** Permission to reconnect later; loop back with referrer.
**Timing:** Late morning, Tuesday-Thursday.

## Sequence Notes

- **Speed matters:** Referrals have a half-life. Send Email 1 within 24 hours
- **Always credit the referrer:** Mention their name in every email
- **Keep the referrer updated:** Let them know the outcome (they invested social capital)
- **Tone:** Warmer and more confident than cold outreach — you've earned trust
- **If no response after 3 emails:** Update the referrer; ask if a different approach would work
- **Thank the referrer:** Regardless of outcome, thank [REFERRER_NAME] for the introduction
- **CRM tracking:** Tag as "referral" source with referrer name for attribution
